Unique Display Case

The Display Case is distinguished by its straight lines and modern style. Its sleek design promotes simplicity and strikes the right balance between practicality and aesthetics. The options offered are incredibly well thoughtout and increase the flexibility offered by this “unique” display case.

2022, Belitung Maritime Museum

Exhibition Drawers

The table case may be supplied with a single full-width exhibition drawer or two side-by-side drawers. These drawers include a secured and removable glass top. They are available in a variety of laminate finishes to blend with your gallery spaces.

Enchance your exhibition display surface and create interaction with your visitors

Opening Mechanism and Security

The table case offers a clamshell opening mechanism for easy access to your collections. It is built with laminated safety glass which will protect your artefacts from exposure to UV rays. Your display case will also meet the highest level of security with its concealed Cam Lock.

A simple, secure and safe display case that can easily be operated by one person

LED Lighting

A flexible LED system with dimmable and adjustable mini spotlight fixtures on a low voltage power track can easily be added or removed to meet your changing display needs.

A very adaptable LED system that will brilliantly highlight your artifacts.

Delivery and Maintenance

The Display Case are shipped completely assembled and require minimal maintenance, thanks to their sealed, dust-proof display enclosures.
